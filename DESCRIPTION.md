# KeepUp - Never Lose Your Place in Sequential Content

Stay on top of everything you're following. KeepUp is your personal tracker for sequential content across the web—whether you're reading manga chapters, watching anime episodes, taking online courses, or following webcomics.

## Key Features

- **Smart Bookmarking**: Save and organize links to chapters, lectures, and episodes you're currently following
- **Automatic Tracking**: Keep track of where you left off with automatic update notifications
- **One-Click Access**: Instantly jump to your next unwatched episode, unread chapter, or pending lecture
- **Organized & Simple**: Manage all your sequential content in one intuitive interface
- **Never Miss Updates**: Get notified when new content is available in your tracked series

## Perfect For

- Manga and comic readers
- Anime and series enthusiasts
- Online learners and course takers
- Podcast followers
- Anyone tracking episodic content
